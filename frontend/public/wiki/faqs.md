# FAQs

Common questions about the WWE 2K League site.

## Standings and rankings

**How are standings ordered?**  
Players are ranked by wins first, then by win percentage, then by fewer losses. Draws count in your record but don’t change the main tie-breakers.

**What’s the difference between All-Time and Season standings?**  
All-Time shows every match ever recorded. Season standings show only matches that are part of the selected season. Use the season dropdown on the Standings page to switch.

## Championships

**What does “Vacant” mean?**  
A title is vacant when no one is currently set as champion (for example before the first defense or after a storyline vacating).

**Where do I see past champions?**  
Open a championship and use the history or “Past champions” section to see previous title holders and reign lengths.

## Events and matches

**Where did the Matches page go?**  
Matches are now under **Events**. Use the Events calendar to see what’s coming and open an event to see its matches and results.

## Tournaments

**What’s single-elimination?**  
You’re out after one loss. The bracket advances until one winner remains.

**What’s round-robin?**  
Everyone faces everyone. Points (e.g. 2 for a win, 1 for a draw) determine the final order.
