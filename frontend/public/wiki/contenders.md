# Contender Rankings

The Contenders page shows the official #1 contender rankings for each championship.

## What Are Contender Rankings?

Contender rankings are automatically calculated based on recent match performance. Each championship has its own rankings, showing who has earned the right to challenge for the title. Rankings may be locked to specific divisions, meaning only players in the same division as the championship can contend.

## Reading the Rankings

| Label | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Rank** | The player's contender position (#1 is next in line for a title shot) |
| **Score** | A calculated ranking score based on recent wins and performance |
| **Win %** | The player's win percentage during the ranking period |
| **Streak** | Current winning or losing streak |
