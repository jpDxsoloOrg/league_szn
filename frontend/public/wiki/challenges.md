# Challenges

*This feature is for wrestlers. It may be enabled or disabled by your league.*

As a wrestler you can issue, accept, decline, or counter challenges to set up matches with other players.

## Challenge Board

Open **Challenges** from the navigation menu to see the Challenge Board. It lists active challenges you've sent or received. Use the filters (Active, Pending, Accepted, Recent) to find specific challenges.

## Issuing a challenge

Click **Issue Challenge** to open the Promo Editor in call-out mode. Write a short call-out promo and submit it; a challenge is created and linked to your promo. You can also go to Promos and create a call-out promo from there.

## Responding to a challenge

Open a challenge from the board or from My Profile to view details. You can **Accept** (to schedule a match), **Decline**, or **Counter** (issue a challenge back with your own stipulation).

## Challenge statuses

Challenges can be **Pending** (awaiting response), **Accepted** (match to be scheduled), **Declined**, **Countered**, **Scheduled** (match booked), **Expired**, or **Cancelled**.
