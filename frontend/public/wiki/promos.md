# Promos

*This feature is for wrestlers and may be enabled or disabled by your league.*

Cut promos to build storylines, call out opponents, or respond to other wrestlers. Promos appear in the feed and can be linked to challenges.

## Promo feed

Open **Promos** from the navigation menu to see the promo feed. Pinned promos appear at the top. Scroll to read open-mic promos and call-outs. To read responses to a promo, open that promo — its thread view shows the original plus every reply.

## Creating a promo

Click **Cut a Promo** to open the editor. Give your promo a title and write the body. Use **@WrestlerName** to mention other wrestlers.

Choose a type: **Open Mic** (general promo), **Call-Out** (challenge another wrestler; this can create a challenge), or **Response** (reply to another wrestler's promo).

## Reacting to promos

On any promo you can leave a reaction. Reactions are visible to everyone and add to the storyline.

## Call-outs and challenges

A call-out promo can create a challenge automatically. The challenged wrestler can accept, decline, or counter from the challenge. Once a match is scheduled from a challenge, it may appear on the event card and in your match history.
