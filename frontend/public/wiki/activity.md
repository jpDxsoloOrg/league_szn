# Activity Feed

The Activity feed shows recent league happenings in one place: match results, title changes, season events, tournament updates, challenges, and promos.

## What It Shows

Items in the feed can include:

- **Match results** — Completed matches with winners and, when relevant, championship implications.
- **Championship changes** — New champions crowned or titles vacated.
- **Season events** — Season start/end or other season-related updates.
- **Tournament results** — Tournament matches and winners.
- **Challenges** — New challenges issued or accepted.
- **Promos** — Promo posts from wrestlers.

## Order and Filtering

- **Order** — Items are ordered by time, newest first, so you see the latest activity at the top.
- **Type filter** — Use the filter (if available) to show only certain types (e.g. matches, championships, challenges) so you can focus on what you care about.

## Related Pages

- **Events** — For the full calendar and match cards, see the [Events](events) article.
- **Championships** — For title history and current champions, see [Championships](championships).
- **Standings** — For rankings and records, see [Standings](standings).

The Activity feed is a quick way to stay updated; use the links in each item or the main menu to dive into details.
