# Dashboard

The Dashboard is your league overview. From here you can see what’s happening at a glance and jump to the main areas of the site.

## What You’ll See

- **Current Champions** — A strip of active championships and their current title holders. Click a championship to see its history and details.
- **Upcoming Events** — Next events on the calendar. Use this to see when the next show or PPV is and open it for the full match card.
- **Recent Results** — The latest completed matches with winners and outcomes. Keeps you up to date without opening the full Events or Matches views.
- **Season Progress** — If a season is active, you’ll see how the season is going (e.g. matches played, standings snapshot).
- **Quick Stats** — Highlights such as most wins, longest streak, newest champion, or similar league stats.
- **Active Challenges** — A link or summary to current challenges so you can respond or issue new ones.

## Getting Around

From the Dashboard you can reach:

- **Standings** — Full league rankings (all-time or by season).
- **Events** — Calendar and event details with matches and results.
- **Championships** — All titles, current champions, and history.
- **Statistics** — Leaderboards, records, rivalries, and more.

Use the main menu or the links on the Dashboard to navigate. For more detail on any section, see [Getting started](getting-started) and the other Wiki articles (Help menu).
