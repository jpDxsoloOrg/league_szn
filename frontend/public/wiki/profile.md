# My Profile

*This feature is available when you're logged in as a wrestler.*

Your personal wrestler profile page shows your complete league history and statistics.

## What You Can See

- Your wrestler name, image, and division assignment
- All-time win/loss/draw record and win percentage
- Per-season records for every season you've competed in
- Your current championship holdings and contender status

From My Profile you can also quickly see your sent and received challenges and open the full Challenge Board.
