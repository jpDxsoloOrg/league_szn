# Getting Started

Welcome to the WWE 2K League site. This short guide helps you find your way around.

## What you can do

- **View standings** — See league rankings, wins, losses, and divisions on the Standings page.
- **Browse championships** — Check current title holders and championship history.
- **See events** — View the calendar, event details, and results.
- **Tournaments** — Follow single-elimination and round-robin tournaments.

## Finding help

- Use **Help** in the main menu to browse the Wiki.
- The Wiki has articles on standings, seasons, championships, events, tournaments, and more, plus FAQs.
- Switch language with the language control in the header (English / Deutsch).

## Next steps

- If you're a **wrestler**, log in to see your profile, issue or respond to challenges, and post promos.
- If **Fantasy** is enabled, log in with a Fantasy account to make picks and view leaderboards.
- Use **Stats** to explore leaderboards, records, and head-to-head data (when the feature is on).
