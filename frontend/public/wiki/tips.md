# Tips for Following the League

Quick tips to get the most out of the league site.

1. **Check Standings Regularly** — See who's climbing the rankings.
2. **Watch for Championship Matches** — These are marked with a special badge.
3. **Follow Tournament Progress** — Tournaments often determine championship opportunities.
4. **Review Match History** — Filter by "Completed" to catch up on results you missed.
5. **Track Contender Rankings** — See who's next in line for a championship opportunity.
6. **Browse Events** — Check out upcoming PPVs and weekly shows for the full match cards.
