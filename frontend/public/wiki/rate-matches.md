# Rate Matches

After a Match has been marked Completed by a GM, signed-in users can rate it from 0.5 to 5 stars.

## How it works

- Open a completed match (Events → pick an event, or use the Match Search page).
- Hover the star strip below the result to preview a rating, then click to commit.
- Each user can rate a match **once**. After you rate, the widget locks and shows your rating.
- The match's overall star rating is the average of all submitted user ratings, rounded to the nearest half star.

## Why it matters

When a match belongs to a rivalry, every rating you give nudges the rivalry's **heat**. Highly-rated matches make a rivalry hotter; poorly-rated ones cool it down.

See also: [How rivalry heat works](rivalry-heat-explained).
