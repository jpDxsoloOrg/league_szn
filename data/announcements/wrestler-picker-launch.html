Heads up — picking your wrestler is now a dropdown, not a text box.

We just rolled out a managed wrestler roster. That means every wrestler in the league is a curated entry with a promotion and an overall cap, and two players can't accidentally end up claiming the same wrestler.

What's new

  • Sign up: new players pick their wrestler from the roster dropdown during registration.
  • My Profile: you can change your current wrestler and set an alternate from the same dropdown, grouped by promotion.
  • Availability: wrestlers already taken by another player are hidden from the dropdown automatically — no more guessing who's free.
  • Post-signup landing page: new players get a welcome screen with the house rules and a heads-up that they're pending a manager's approval.

What you need to do

  1. Head to My Profile and click Edit.
  2. If your profile is missing a roster wrestler, you'll see a banner reminding you to pick one. Open the Current Wrestler dropdown and choose yours.
  3. Optional: pick an Alternate Wrestler the same way.
  4. Hit Save and you're done.

On the Standings page, any player still missing a roster pick shows a small "Needs wrestler" pill so managers can spot profiles that need cleanup.

Don't see your wrestler in the dropdown? They might not be in the roster yet — message a GM and we'll add them.
