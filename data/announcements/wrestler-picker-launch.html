Roster Upgrade Is Live

April 23, 2026
Pick Your Wrestler From the Roster No more free-text — the roster is official.

Every wrestler in League SZN is now a curated roster entry with a promotion and an overall cap. Two players can't accidentally claim the same wrestler, and managers can finally see who's booked to whom at a glance.

What's New
  • Sign up: new players pick their wrestler from the roster dropdown.
  • My Profile: swap your current wrestler and set an alternate from the same dropdown, grouped by promotion.
  • Availability: wrestlers already claimed by someone else are hidden — no more guessing.
  • Welcome page: new signups land on a rules page after confirming their email.
What You Need To Do
  1. Open My Profile and hit Edit.
  2. If you see a Pick your wrestler banner at the top, open the Current Wrestler dropdown and choose yours.
  3. Optionally pick an Alternate Wrestler the same way.
  4. Save — the banner disappears, and your wrestler name shows up everywhere it should.

On the Standings page, any player still missing a roster pick shows a small Needs wrestler pill — managers can spot the cleanup list at a glance.

“Don't see your wrestler in the dropdown? Ping a GM — we'll get them added to the roster.”
